Welcome to Energy Club

We are a team of inspired tech-enthusiasts who want to build a productive community

Our Vision

To bring together CET students, scholars, alumni and professors of all levels irrespective of discipline, who are interested in energy conservation and sustainability issues.

About us

Our Aim

To initiate, promote and support all technical and non-technical endeavours such as projects, competitions, workshops, awareness drives etc. So that there can be efficient usage of energy, awareness and sustainability at their core.

Why us?

  1. TURNING CONCERN INTO ACTION
  2. EXPLORE AND INNOVATE
  3. LEARNING FROM EXPERIENCE
  4. EXPERTISE YOUR PASSION
  5. WORK TOGETHER-VALUE TEAMWORK
  6. MAKE A DIFFERENCE
